How It Works
1. Print or display a Hiro/Kanji marker image on screen.
2. Open this page on a phone or laptop with a camera.
3. Allow camera permission when prompted.
4. Point camera at the marker — video plays floating over it in AR.
5. Move the camera away to pause. Return to resume.
